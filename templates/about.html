About myTZStudies

Free exam resources for every Tanzanian student — no matter where you are or how much money you have.

Our Story

TZStudies was built by a Tanzanian student who understood firsthand how hard it is to find good study materials. Past exam papers were either impossible to find online, hidden behind expensive websites, or only available if you knew the right people.

The goal was simple — build one place where every student in Tanzania, whether in Dar es Salaam or a small village in Mbeya, could access past papers, answer keys, and study help completely for free.

What started as a small personal project has grown into a platform serving thousands of students preparing for Standard 4, Form 2, Form 4, and Form 6 national examinations. Every paper, every answer key, and every feature on this site is free because we believe education should never have a price tag.

What We Offer

Past Exam Papers

Hundreds of past papers from national examinations going back multiple years. Download them as PDFs directly to your phone or computer.

Answer Keys

Detailed answer keys with step-by-step explanations written in simple language. Every answer explains not just what is correct but why it is correct.

Find a Tutor

Browse verified tutors across Tanzania by subject. Connect directly with experienced teachers who know the national curriculum inside out.

AI Study Assistant

Ask any exam question and get an instant detailed explanation. Available any time of day or night — even when your teacher is not available.

All Levels Covered

Resources for Standard 4, Form 2, Form 4, and Form 6. Whether you are preparing for your first national exam or your last, TZStudies has you covered.

Works on Any Phone

TZStudies is designed mobile-first because we know most students in Tanzania use phones, not laptops. Fast, lightweight, and works even on slow internet connections.
